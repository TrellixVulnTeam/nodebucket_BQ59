About Us

Nodebucket is a productivity application that was created in September 2021 in San Antonio, Texas, with the goal of helping people manage their every day lives.

If you're a student, you've probably struggled with keeping track of your weekly assignments. If you run a small business, you may forget to write down something important that needs to be done by the end of the day. If you're anything like us, you probably write down your grocery list and forget to bring the list with you to the store. Does your boss ever give you a bunch of things to do, but you simply can't remember what they are throughout the day?

If you've experienced any of that, then Nodebucket is or you. We've created what we believe is the most well-rounded app for task management, but we also believe it is simple enough that anyone can use it.

It's hard to stay motivated and be productive when you it feels like your work is never-ending. With Nodebucket, you can add tasks to your ToDo list. As you complete each task, it gets moved to your Done list, which means that your ToDo list is visually shrinking. There's nothing greater than seeing your work being completed.

We're confident that our simple design and great support team will keep you coming back to Nodebucket for a very long time. Get rid of that pen and paper. Your new favorite app for task management is here. Welcome to Nodebucket.
